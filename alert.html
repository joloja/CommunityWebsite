The Raritan Township Police Department serves as the vigilant guardians of our community, committed to ensuring safety, security, and trust. With a dedicated team of officers, they uphold the highest standards of integrity and professionalism while serving the residents of Raritan Township. Their mission encompasses proactive crime prevention, responsive emergency services, and fostering strong community partnerships. Committed to excellence, they work tirelessly to maintain a safe environment where residents can thrive and feel secure.


Ways the police department can help

  • Patrol and Surveillance: Increase patrols in areas with recent burglary reports to deter criminal activity and provide a visible presence.
  • Investigation: Conduct thorough investigations into reported burglaries, collecting evidence and following up on leads to apprehend suspects.
  • Crime Analysis: Use data analysis to identify patterns or trends in burglaries, helping to allocate resources effectively and prevent further incidents.
  • Community Outreach: Offer educational programs or workshops to educate residents on burglary prevention measures, such as securing homes and recognizing suspicious behavior.
  • Collaboration with Residents: Encourage residents to report any suspicious activities promptly and provide information that could aid investigations.
  • Neighborhood Watch Support: Assist in the formation and maintenance of neighborhood watch groups, providing guidance, resources, and communication channels.
  • Victim Support Services: Offer support and resources for burglary victims, including guidance on filing reports and accessing support networks.
  • Technology Integration: Utilize technology like CCTV camera networks or home security system partnerships to enhance surveillance and evidence gathering.
  • Public Awareness Campaigns: Launch campaigns to raise awareness about burglary risks and preventive measures through social media, community events, and newsletters.
  • Collaboration with Other Agencies: Work with other law enforcement agencies or task forces to share information and coordinate efforts in tackling burglary-related issues.
